# One-Pager: Lolly "Always-On" Payment Resilience

## The Hook
Zero missed sales. Zero fan frustration. Even when the network goes dark.

## The Problem
In the high-pressure environment of a stadium halftime, a 60-second network glitch doesn't just delay sales—it kills them. Current "Store and Forward" (Offline) modes are blunt instruments, often resulting in 5-10% post-event fraud or decline rates, making CFOs hesitant to enable them at scale.

## The Solution: Lolly Edge-Guard
A mission-critical payment architecture that shifts authorization intelligence from the cloud to the terminal.
1.  **Intelligent Edge Processing:** Local risk assessment based on terminal history, velocity checks, and transaction limits.
2.  **Seamless Transition:** Terminals switch to Edge-Guard mode in <500ms when heartbeat latency exceeds 2000ms.
3.  **Risk-Managed Offline Approvals:** Dynamic limits (e.g., $50 for low-risk, $20 for high-risk BINS) to keep revenue flowing while capping exposure.

## The Value
- **For the Venue:** Recovers the ~20% of revenue at risk during peak outages.
- **For the Fan:** Maintains the "Invisible Infrastructure" experience; no "System Down" signs.
- **For Lolly:** Positions us as the only enterprise POS capable of handling mission-critical, Tier-1 global events.
