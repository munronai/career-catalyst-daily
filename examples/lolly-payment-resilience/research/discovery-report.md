# Phase 1: Research & Discovery - Payment Resilience at the Edge

## Problem Statement
In high-volume hospitality environments (stadia, festivals, major events), network latency or total outages translate directly into irreversible revenue loss. With the industry trend toward 100% cashless venues, a payment failure is an operational total-stop.

## Competitive Landscape & Benchmarks
- **Current Standard:** Most POS systems offer a binary "Offline Mode" which stores card data blindly, leading to high post-event decline rates (often 5-10% of offline volume).
- **Lolly Advantage (Proposed):** Intelligent Risk-Based Offline Approvals. Using local edge-processing to evaluate risk (e.g., transaction history, velocity, bin checks) before "approving" an offline transaction.
