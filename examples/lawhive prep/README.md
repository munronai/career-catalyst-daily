The Role
As a Product Manager, you will own the delivery of our AI operating system and core platforms. You will sit directly with our engineers and AI team every day, turning high-level business goals into technical requirements.

Your job is to figure out the "what" and the "how" so the engineering team can focus on the build. This means writing clear product specifications, mapping out system logic, defining edge cases, and removing roadblocks so the technical team can ship code quickly and reliably.

Partner directly with engineering and AI teams to translate complex, high-level objectives into deeply technical requirements and delivery plans.

Dissect intricate system challenges and workflows to ensure engineers are unblocked and building the right things efficiently.

Focus heavily on delivery, technical execution, and analytical/strategic platform work.

Drive the core capabilities of our Lawrence AI engine and platform architecture, ensuring scalability and exceptional performance.

Create and maintain tight, delivery-oriented feedback loops with the technical team.

Immerse yourself in the legal domain, dedicating yourself to a steep learning curve to understand the unique constraints and workflows of the legal sector.

Work with the leadership team on product strategy, vision, priorities, and technical roadmap across the entire business.

Use data, analytical tools, and a deep understanding of system architecture to inform product decisions.

What You'll Bring
A deeply technical product mindset. You don’t need to write code, but you must know exactly how to work with engineers, speak their language fluently, and dissect problems into technical requirements.

Exceptional experience working on relevant, complex technical platforms and environments.

A highly delivery-oriented approach with a proven track record of shipping complex, high-quality technical products in fast-moving environments.

Strong analytical and strategic skills, capable of balancing immediate engineering needs with long-term platform architecture.

The adaptability and rigor to ramp up quickly in a complex legal sector, while immediately adding value on the technical delivery side.

Deep understanding of modern product development practices and end-to-end delivery alongside engineers.
