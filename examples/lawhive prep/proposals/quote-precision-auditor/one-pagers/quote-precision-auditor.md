# One-Pager: The Quote Precision AI Auditor

**The Problem:** Lawhive's promise of "Transparent Fixed Fees" is undermined by "Pricing Latency"—the 24-48 hour window where initial quotes are re-evaluated by humans due to missed complexity during automated intake. This leads to user churn, negative sentiment (Trustpilot 1-star reviews), and internal inefficiency.

**The Solution:** An "Edge-Case Detection Engine" integrated into the intake flow. The Auditor uses **Historical Matter Analysis** and **Latent Signal Extraction** (e.g., detecting multiple defendants in a witness statement) to flag high-variance cases *before* a quote is issued.

**The Strategic Value:**
1. **Reduce Churn:** Eliminate "Quote Correction" events that drive user dissatisfaction.
2. **Maximize AI Dividend:** Ensure complexity is priced correctly so that AI-driven lawyer efficiency translates into firm profit.
3. **Build Trust:** Position Lawhive as the most reliable "infrastructure" for legal work, matching the "Shopify for Law" vision.

## ⚙️ How it Works (High-Level)
1. **Intake Signal Capture:** Lawrence (AI Agent) extracts entity and complexity signals from uploaded documents.
2. **Variance Prediction:** Auditor compares signals against Lawhive's historical data for similar cases.
3. **Routing Logic:** Cases with >20% probability of a price shift are auto-routed for immediate "Junior Lawyer Audit" during the intake flow.
4. **Transparent Quote:** Users are issued a quote with explicit "Complexity Badges" (e.g., "Standard," "High-Entity Count," "Multi-Jurisdiction") to justify pricing.
