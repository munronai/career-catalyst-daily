# One-Pager: Quick Win 2 — Range-Based Expectation Management

**The Problem:** Instant quotes for high-complexity or high-volume matters often lead to inaccurate expectations. When the quote inevitably changes, the user feels the system is "broken."

**The Solution:** For matters flagged as "Complex" (e.g., multi-party, high document volume), we move from an **Instant Quote** to a **Range Estimate + SLA.** We provide a likely range (e.g., £800 – £1,200) and commit to a final verified price within 4 working hours.

**The Strategic Value:**
1. **Transparency over Speed:** We acknowledge complexity upfront, which signals professional expertise rather than automated guesswork.
2. **Operational Breathability:** Gives human solicitors time to review high-volume intake periods without the pressure of an "Instant" clock ticking against an inaccurate number.
3. **Professionalism:** In high-stakes law, "Instant" can sometimes feel "Cheap." A range + human review increases the perceived value of the legal work.

---

## 🚀 Impact (Predicted 3-Month Success)
- **100% Elimination** of "Bait and Switch" complaints for complex matters.
- **95% SLA Adherence** for final quote issuance.
- **Improved Lawyer NPS:** Solicitors feel supported by the system rather than being forced to correct "bad" AI quotes.
