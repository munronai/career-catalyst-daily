# Executive Review: Lolly Connect
**Reviewer:** CEO/CFO Persona
**Date:** 2024-05-23

## Verdict: STRONG APPROVE
This proposal addresses a critical strategic gap in our enterprise offering.

### Strengths
- **Alignment with Enterprise Growth:** The focus on SAP/Fourth/Procure Wizard is spot on. These are the "door openers" for the large catering contracts we are targeting.
- **Monetization Strategy:** I like the tiered pricing approach. Moving toward "API-as-a-Product" aligns with our goal of increasing recurring software revenue vs. one-time hardware sales.
- **Risk Mitigation:** The "Bank-Grade" approach to security (OAuth 2.0, MTLS) is essential for maintaining our reputation in the high-compliance Education and NHS sectors.

### Areas for Improvement
- **Sales Enablement:** We need to ensure the Sales team understands how to sell "Connectivity" as a feature. I’d like to see a "Sales Playbook" added to the roadmap.
- **Partner Quality:** How do we ensure 3rd-party apps don't degrade the Lolly brand experience? We need a clear "Certification Program" defined early.

## ROI Assessment
Highly favorable. The reduction in bespoke engineering time alone covers the initial development costs within 12 months.
