# Presentation: Lolly Connect (API Partner Platform)

## Slide 1: The Vision Hook
**Title:** From Hardware Vendor to Data Platform
**Subtitle:** Unlocking Lolly's Ecosystem through API-First Innovation
**Key Message:** By building a self-serve partner platform, Lolly can move from a "Single-Product" vendor to a "Multi-Service" platform, increasing ACV and reducing churn.

---

## Slide 2: The Data-Backed Problem
**Title:** Fragmented Data is Killing Efficiency
**Data Point 1:** Enterprise operators use an average of **12+ disconnected software tools**.
**Data Point 2:** Bespoke integrations currently take **4-8 weeks** of manual engineering effort.
**Insight:** Lolly's "closed" ecosystem is a bottleneck for enterprise sales. We are leaving revenue on the table and ceding market share to "open" competitors like Toast.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution & Strategy
**Title:** Lolly Connect: The Hospitality Operating System
**The Solution:** A developer-first platform featuring a self-serve portal, unified API schema, and event-driven webhooks.
**The Strategy:**
1. **Unify:** Create the "Lolly Unified Hospitality API."
2. **Expose:** Launch the Developer Portal & Sandbox.
3. **Ecosystem:** Build turnkey integrations with Fourth, SAP, and Procure Wizard.
