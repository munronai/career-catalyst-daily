# Strategy & Vision: Genomics for Neighbourhood Health 2026

## 1. The Strategic Context
The NHS is pivoting towards a "Neighbourhood Health" model—decentralizing care from hospitals to community-based teams. Genomics England (GEL) must move from being a research-heavy sequence provider to an **Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS)** provider for these teams.

## 2. The Vision: "Upstream" Genomics
By 2026, genomics will no longer be the "last resort" in a specialist clinic. It will be the "first signal" in a GP practice.
*   **From Reactive to Proactive:** Instead of waiting for a patient to present with symptoms, GaaS enables GPs to identify high-risk individuals (e.g., Lynch Syndrome, FH) within their community using Polygenic Risk Scores (PRS).
*   **From Specialist to Generalist:** GaaS abstracts away the complexity of genomic interpretation, providing GPs with clear, clinical "next steps."

## 3. The Multiplier Effect
GaaS acts as a multiplier for three key NHS pillars:
1.  **Prevention:** Identifying cancer risks years before onset.
2.  **Safety:** Eliminating the "trial and error" of prescribing through Pharmacogenomics.
3.  **Equity:** Ensuring that genomic medicine is available to all patients through their local GP, not just those near major teaching hospitals.

## 4. Key Strategic Assumptions
*   **Assumption 1:** By 2026, FHIR R4 is the universal standard across major GP EHR vendors (EMIS, TPP).
*   **Assumption 2:** The "Generation Study" (newborn sequencing) will have created a massive data lake of genomic variants that need Primary Care management.
*   **Assumption 3:** The NHS App will become the primary portal for patient-facing genomic insights.

## 5. Strategic Roadmap (The 2026 Path)
*   **Q4 2024 (Foundation):** Launch FHIR Gateway Beta with 5 pilot PCNs (Primary Care Networks). Focus exclusively on Pharmacogenomics (PGx).
*   **Q2 2025 (Expansion):** Integrate National Genomic Test Directory for digital ordering. Add rare disease "red flag" alerts.
*   **Q1 2026 (Mainstreaming):** National rollout of "Neighbourhood Risk Dashboards" for health leads. Full integration with the NHS App.

## 6. Competitive Advantage: Why GEL?
Genomics England is the only entity with the scale of data (5PB+) and the clinical trust to bridge the gap between research-grade sequencing and frontline primary care. GaaS is the digital conduit for that trust.
