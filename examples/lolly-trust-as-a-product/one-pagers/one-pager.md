# One-Pager: Lolly "Trust-as-a-Product" (ISO 42001)

## The Hook
Lolly is the first UK hospitality tech firm to achieve ISO 42001. We aren't just selling AI; we are selling **Certified Trust**. This proposal outlines how to weaponise our accreditation to dominate the Enterprise (University & Stadia) market by removing the "Ethical Friction" that stalls high-value deals.

## The Problem
Enterprise procurement (especially in Universities) is increasingly blocked by **AI Compliance Anxiety**.
1. **The Compliance Wall:** IT departments reject AI tray scanning/facial verification due to GDPR and bias concerns.
2. **The "Black Box" Fear:** Student Unions and EDI boards view AI as a threat to privacy and fairness.
3. **The Sales Lag:** Lolly sales teams spend months answering custom security/ethics questionnaires for every major tender.

## The Solution: Trust-as-a-Product
Shift the narrative from "We have AI" to "We have the only **Certified Responsible AI** in the sector."
- **ISO 42001 Portal:** A self-serve portal for Enterprise IT teams to download our AI Impact Assessments, Bias Mitigation reports, and Data Governance manifests.
- **Trust-Verified APIs:** "Governance Headers" in our APIs that provide metadata about AI confidence scores and bias checks for every automated decision.
- **"Certified by LollySense" Branding:** A consumer-facing trust mark (stickers on kiosks, badges in-app) that reassures students their data is ethical and secure.

## The Value (Incoming PM Perspective)
- **Reduced Sales Cycle:** Cut technical/legal review time by 30% through standardized ISO 42001 documentation.
- **Tender Win Rate:** Positioning Lolly as the only "Safe" choice for public-sector and high-compliance institutions.
- **Platform Integrity:** Leveraging my SWIFT/Metro Bank background (ISO 20022/Open Banking) to treat AI governance with the same rigor as financial transactions.

## Success Metric
- 100% of University tenders in 2026 feature ISO 42001 as a mandatory requirement (influenced by Lolly's lobbying and standard-setting).
